OpenRefine: it is a powerful, free, open-source tool designed for cleaning, transforming, and analyzing messy data, particularly useful for working with structured data like CSV, Excel, JSON.
Advantages:
   1.Efficient Data Cleaning and Transformation
   2.Undo/Redo Functionality
   3.OpenRefine operates entirely on the user's local machine, ensuring that sensitive data is not transmitted over the internet
   4.Users can extend OpenRefine's functionality through extensions and integrate it with external web services
Disadvantages:
	1.Limited Support for Unstructured Data
	2.Performance may degrade with very large datasets
	3.Requires technical knowledge for advanced integrations
	4.Lacks real-time collaboration features
